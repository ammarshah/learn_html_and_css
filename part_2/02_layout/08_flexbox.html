Using flex:

It's a shorthand property of the combination of flex-basis, flex-grow and flex-shrink.

It takes 1-3 values as mentioned below:

flex: 1; /* Applies to flex-grow */

flex: 1 1; /* Applies to flex-grow and flex-shrink, respectively */

flex: 1 20rem; /* Applies to flex-grow and flex-basis, respectively */

flex: 1 1 20rem; /* Applies to flex-grow, flex-shrink and flex-basis, respectively */
